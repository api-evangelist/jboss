Finding product-market fit (PMF) is arguably the most critical challenge faced by startups. Navigating the path to PMF can often feel like moving through a labyrinth, with each turn posing potential setbacks or breakthroughs. The PMF framework is designed to guide startups through the various stages of validating and scaling their product in the market.
